Iced Latte

A Costa iced latte is a refreshing cold drink made with shots of espresso, fresh milk, and a touch of sweetness. Perfect for hot summer days or as a mid-day pick-me-up.

Latte

Indulge in the creamy, smooth taste of a Costa latte - a rich and luxurious blend of espresso and velvety milk. Served with a beautiful, delicate foam and finished with a sprinkling of chocolate powder, our Costa latte is the perfect pick-me-up to savor throughout the day. Treat yourself to the ultimate coffee experience today!

Latte in a Can

Experience the rich, smooth taste of a Costa latte in the convenience of a can! Made with premium espresso and creamy milk, our canned Costa latte is the perfect on-the-go pick-me-up for busy mornings or when you need a delicious coffee break. Savor the authentic taste of Costa in every sip, anytime, anywhere.
